Controller Designer

Instructions

Click and drag joysticks and buttons into the controller area to design a controller.

Drag any unwanted elements over the trash square to remove them from the design

Click submit to produce a list of the control type and position. Position is currently in pixels but can easily be millimeters, inches, parsecs, etc...
