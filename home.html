Axis Pro Designer

Instructions

Use one of the presets to get a jump on your design or click and drag buttons to create something from scratch!

Once you're done, enter your name and a contact email so that we can collaborate and fine tune your work.

Your design will serve as a starting point. An engineer will examine your work and make small tweaks to allow to appropriate spacing
between controls. Our engineer will confirm the final design and any other changes you would like before putting it into production.

Playstation control symbols are shown before Xbox control symbols. E.g. 'O' (Playstation) is listed before 'B' (Xbox) for the red button
